Workflow of some common process are described below.

REGISTRATION

Signup process in the app is a two step verification process in which a user is needs to have a valid EMAIL and a valid Phone Number . Phone number is verified by an otp and an email verifiation link for account verification is sent to the user for the complete account setup . A user register and completes his email verification he can login to freelance

REQUIRED AN EMAIL ID PHONE NUMBER

LOGIN

The login process is a simple username/password combination which lets you access your account . After the account is Logged in a list of categories is given to the user from which he can choose the category in which he belongs so that he is notfiied when a project of his category comes up . P.S. this category selection is a one time process.

HIRE MODE

To hire a person a user must be in hiremode . Hire mode can be set by a toggle in the app . A user can either hire a single person or post a project and hire a group of people . For the purpose of hiring a form wih the relevant details is filled up which contains all the details of the work with tentaive date of accomplishment . When hired for a job the worker gets notified for the job and he needs to accept or reject the offer . Once accepted he can hired by the employer with an agreement

WORK MODE

In worker mode a person can work as a freelancer . A user in worker mode can receive work based on his domain . Once he/he accepts a project the employee and the employer can go for futher discussion on a chatting platform for further negotiation . If the negotiation goes well the employer can hire him with agreement of accomplishment .

A worker can work for multiple jobs and can monitor his earnings in the dashboard . Freelance is neither subjected to the quality of work delivered nor the amount for negotiation of work . However the employer can have feedbacks on a worker based on his performace and can rate his work.

A worker can be certified by the Freelance authority on successful verification of the documents enlisted in his accunt and can be promoted based on that.
